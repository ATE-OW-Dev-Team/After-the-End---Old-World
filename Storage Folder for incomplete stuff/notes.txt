Angrezi/Anglo-Indian sound changes (from baseline English):
Y to I
[Y or AY] to EI (in case of "ai" sound)
I to E
M to N
O to A
O to AU (in case of "au" sound)
A to E
C to S (in case of "s" sound)
C to K (in case of "k" sound)

AI to EH
